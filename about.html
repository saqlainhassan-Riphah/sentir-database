About the Project

SentiR Database is an open-access academic repository developed to support research in sentiment analysis, corpus linguistics, computational linguistics, and discourse studies.

The platform provides freely accessible datasets and research resources for students, researchers, and educators around the world.

The repository particularly focuses on social media discourse, public opinion analysis, and computational approaches to language research.
