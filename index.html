Welcome

The SentiR Database is a publicly available academic repository designed to support research in sentiment analysis, corpus linguistics, and discourse studies.

Purpose

This platform aims to provide high-quality CSV datasets for researchers, students, and educators. All datasets are freely available with proper attribution.

Future Work

In the next phase, datasets will be uploaded in CSV format with metadata, citation details, and licensing information.
