Welcome

The SentiR Database is a publicly available academic repository designed to support research in sentiment analysis, corpus linguistics, and discourse studies.

Purpose

This platform aims to provide high-quality CSV datasets for researchers, students, and educators. All datasets are shared freely with proper attribution.
